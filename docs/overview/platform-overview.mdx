# Platform Overview

momenTUM is a research platform for designing, deploying, and managing ecological momentary assessment studies.

At a high level, the platform consists of:

- the Study Designer, where researchers define study structures
- the mobile app, where participants complete modules and questionnaires
- the backend, which manages study delivery and synchronization
- REDCap integration for research data workflows
- the dashboard, where researchers inspect study responses and participant-level data

This documentation site explains how these components fit together and provides interactive examples for study schemas, demo studies, and participant-facing app flows.
